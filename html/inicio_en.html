What is Cash-Flag?

It is a transactional platform to integrate B2B and B2C services, that seeks to provide affiliated businesses with a tool to increase the Life Time Value of their client portfolio, offering them benefits for being frequent consumers, as well as an alternative means of payment and provide other services to facilitate business operations.

In its first stage, Cash-Flag is a loyalty program supported by the issuance of prepaid and gift cards to be used in merchants affiliated with the platform, generating benefit coupons for users that can be exchanged online.

Cash Flag uses a web application, there, each user will have a card holder and a coupon, where can recharge, redeem coupons and consume online or physically at affiliated merchants.

Affiliate merchants will have a desktop and web application where they can receive card payments from the platform (payment processor), recharge prepaid cards, sell gift cards, validate coupons and register new users.

Additionally, the platform offers a payment button that can be integrated into any web page to process online payments.
