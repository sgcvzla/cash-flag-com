Benefits

For merchant:

  • "Life Time Value" increment, merchant´s client portfolio increases its value by these clients continous repurchases because generating of loyalty and an easy payment process.
  • Knowledge of client´s behavior, the statistical information on the platform will allow the business to identify its consumer´s profile and target promotions to attract traffic generating greater consumption.
  • Reduction of CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost), while community grows the merchant will be able to generate better and more efficient marketing strategies.
  • Reduction of chash flow problems.
  • No membership fees, nor plans, nor periodical payments. (**)
(**) Cash Flag´s income model is based on transactional fees, there could be additional costs if the merchant wishes to personalize any service or B2B / B2C integrations, additionally the merchant must cover the cost of the benefits that they grant to their clients (prizes, discounts) and operating expenses (eg internet data plan).

For consumer:

  • Frequent customer could earn rewards on affiliated businesses.
  • Comfortable and ease of use intuitive application with few steps.
  • It can be an alternative to universal acceptance cash at no cost.
  • No membership nor transaction fees.
